Myths Debunked

MYTH TRUTH
The climate has always been changing While this is indisputable, natural climate changes of the past had changed on a much longer time scale. Today's climate has been changing over a series of decades, which is far too small.
It's just the sun Research indicates that the sun has shown a
cooling trend
Source: Skeptical Science
over the past 20 years. If the sun's temperature is decreasing while the earth's temperature is increasing, it's clear that the sun is not the reason behind climate change.
Human CO2 emissions represent only a very tiny fraction of all CO2 emissions On its own, the earth's natural greenhouse effect takes in solar radiation from the sun, but allows more heat to escape; leaving less re-emitted heat. The human-enhanced greenhouse effect takes in an equal amount of solar radiation, but less heat is able to escape because of the extra greenhouse gases now in the atmosphere because of human activity.
