Good Habits to keep:

  • Use compact fluorescent light bulbs (instead of regular incandescent bulbs)

  • Install a programmable thermostat

  • Move the thermostat temperature 2 degrees down (in the Winter) and 2 degrees up (in the summer)

  • Clean/replace filters in your furnace and AC unit

  • Don’t use standby on appliances: turn on/off

  • Locate fridge and freezer away from cooker/boiler

  • Don’t air out the house for long periods of time: keep windows open for a few minutes, so air doesn’t escape

  • Cover pots when cooking (or use pressure cookers and steamers)

  • Use washing machine or dishwasher when full and with lower heat

  • Take showers instead of baths

  • Use less hot water

  • Use clothesline instead of dryer (when possible)

  • Recycle!

  • Recycle organic waste (compost)

  • Reuse shopping bag

  • Use reusable lunch boxes (instead of paper bags)

  • Plant trees!

  • Use wind/solar energy

  • Buy locally grown and produced foods

  • Buy fresh food instead of frozen

  • Buy organic food

  • Eat less meat

  • Carpool!
